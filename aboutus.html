Our legacy

Sinani is a swiss luxury watch brand, originally created by Lorenzo Sinani in zurich, switzerland in 1847. Trough the late 1800 he cretaed beutiful timepicees for important people around the world. After Sinanis death, his family took over the company and wanted to continue his legacy. For decades now our watches have been through endless of journeys. They have accompanied achievers and the hard working, making our watches a resemblence of sucsess.

Today

Today sinani is the top luxerious watchmaking company in the world. Making watches for every occation. every watch is handcrafted, with passion and desire to fufill the dream of Lorenzo Sinani.
